# Subtext

**Tagline:** Everyone reads the headline. We read the subtext.

**Promise:** Each issue takes a real business/tech story and delivers the product decision underneath it, plus what AI actually does to it. Differentiation = interpretation, not aggregation. The TAKE is the product.

## Reader JTBD

When a big business/tech story breaks, an ambitious operator wants to walk into Monday's meeting sounding like the sharpest person in the room — with a non-obvious, defensible read, not the hype everyone's already repeating. Subtext's job is to hand them ONE sharp read on the week's biggest move — the subtext under the story — plus the AI angle that isn't slop, in under 3 minutes of reading.

## Operating Principles (the bar every issue must clear)

1. **Falsifiable**: if a take is true of any company, it's not a take. Kill it.
2. **Non-consensus-but-right**: if TBOY or the timeline already said it, it fails.
3. **Gated AI lens**: interrogate every story for an AI angle, but only EMIT one if it clears the gate — AI materially changes the unit economics/moat/distribution, OR an AI-native version makes the original look slow, OR it shifts who holds power, OR the consensus "hype vs. not-hype" read is backwards. Otherwise output exactly: "AI lens: skipped — <one-line reason>." A week where only 1 of 3 stories earns an AI take is a GOOD week.
4. **Voice**: confident, concrete, a little irreverent. No buzzword soup. Banned phrase energy: "in today's fast-paced, AI-driven world."

## Format Reference

TBOY (youtube.com/@tboypod) — for narrative STRUCTURE only:
- Hook (grabs attention, sets up the tension)
- Setup (context + the story)
- Metaphor/Insight (the way through it)
- Takeaway (clean, memorable, falsifiable)

Never reuse their wording, metaphors, or headlines. Skeletons are fair game; copy is not.

## Output Shape (Default: LinkedIn Longform)

```
↳ Surface line (the consensus read, marked as struck)
↳ The subtext (the take underneath)
PS take (2–4 sentences, falsifiable)
AI take (2–3 sentences) OR "AI lens: skipped — <reason>"
Takeaway (one line)
Self-score: Differentiation /10 + one-line "could anyone else have written this?" check
```
